INFO : THE WAY WE SOLVE PROBLEMS IN ANY SUBJECT

METHODS :
	
Step 1 : We have to understand the problem well.
		-Understand the data given.
		-Understand the unknown.
		-Understand the condition.

Step 2 : We have to find the relation between the given and the unknown.
	    -Understand the connection between the given and the unknown.
		-Devise a plan according to the relationships and conditions.

Step 3 : Carry out the plan.
	    -We have to carry out the devised plan in the problem.
	
Step 4 : Look back.(verify)
		-Check each and every step carried out in the problem.
		-Check wether the plan suits the prolem.
		-Verify the answer.
